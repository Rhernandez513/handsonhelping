About Hands On Helping

More Info

Hands On Helping is furthering children's education by teaching them English. In countries like Cambodia learning English is often key to finding employment opportunities. We currently have English teachers on site in Cambodia that are on our full time payroll. These teachers also act as interpreters for visitors.
